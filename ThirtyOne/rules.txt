 - 3 players
 - each player starts with 3 cards
 - face cards are 10, ace is 11, and numbers cards are as expected
 - on your turn you can choose from the discard pile or deck and then discard one of your cards
 - game ends once someone gets 31 and calls it or someone knocks
 - once someone knocks, their turn immediately ends, then everyone plays one more turn and the person with the highest score wins
